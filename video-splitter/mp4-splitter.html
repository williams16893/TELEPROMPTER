Free Online MP4 Splitter

Do you want to cut your MP4 video so you can save a part of it as a separate video file? You can use VEED’s online video splitter. Upload your MP4 file, use the video cutter and splitter tool, and export your new MP4 video. You can split your video into as many parts as you want, join clips together, and delete the parts you don’t want to save. VEED is also a free video editor. Edit each clip to make them look stunning. You can add camera effects, filters, and other elements. Add text to your video as well as images, subtitles, shapes, emojis, and more.

How to Use the MP4 Splitter:

1
Select your MP4 file

Upload your MP4 video by clicking on ‘Choose MP4 File’. Select your video from your folder. You can also drag and drop your MP4 file into the box.

2
Click on ‘Split’

Click on the location where you want to split the video and click on the ‘Split’ button right above the timeline. You can also press ‘S’ on your keyboard. You can split your video into several parts. Delete the clips that you don’t want.

3
Export and share

Download your new MP4 video by clicking on ‘Export’. Share it with your friends or post it on social media!
